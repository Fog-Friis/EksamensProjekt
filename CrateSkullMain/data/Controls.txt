Zombie survival:
Movement is controlled by  the 'wasd' buttons. 
Switching weapons is done by pressing the 'e' button.
Using the equipped weapons is done by  pressing the space button.

1v1 deathmatch:
Player one has the same controls as the player in the Zombie survival gamemode.
Player two's movement is controlled by the arrow keys.
Player two can switch weapons by pressing the ',' button.
Player two can use the equipped weapon by pressing '.' button.
